    Are your fans energy-efficient?
    Yes, many of our fan models are energy-efficient and come with BEE star ratings or inverter technology to help reduce electricity bills without compromising on performance.
    Do the fans come with a warranty?
    Absolutely! All of our fans come with a standard 1-year to 3-year manufacturer warranty, depending on the model. Warranty details are mentioned on each product page.
    Can I install the ceiling fan myself?
    While some experienced customers do install fans themselves, we highly recommend using a professional electrician for safe and proper installation, especially for ceiling and wall-mounted fans.
    Do you offer free delivery?
    Yes! We offer free shipping on orders over Rs. 3,000 within Pakistan. For smaller orders, a minimal delivery charge may apply depending on your location.
    What is your return or replacement policy?
    If your fan arrives damaged or not as described, you can request a replacement or refund within 7 days of delivery. Please make sure the product is unused and in its original packaging.
