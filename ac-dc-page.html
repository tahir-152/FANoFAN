    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does the fan run on battery backup?
    On full charge, the fan can run for 3–5 hours depending on the speed setting.
    Can I use the fan while it’s charging?
    Yes, it operates on AC power while the battery charges in the background.
    Does it come with a remote?
    Yes, a smart remote with 7-speed, timer, and night mode is included.
    Is installation included?
    Installation is not included, but any local electrician can set it up easily.
    How much energy does it save?
    It uses up to 65% less energy than traditional ceiling fans thanks to the BLDC motor.
